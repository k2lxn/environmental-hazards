Lead

Inhaling or ingesting even small amounts of lead can cause serious health problems, especially for children. Paint in homes built before 1978 is assumed to be lead-based unless inspection proves otherwise.

Healthy Homes

Healthy Homes is a recent development from HUD and the EPA, reflecting a desire for a more holistic approach to home inspections. Inspectors analyze and prioritize health and injury risks in the home. Environmental Hazards Detection LLC provides the full 29-step inspection.

Stan Reynolds is the principal at Environmental Hazards Detection LLC. Stan has been performing safe, friendly, and compliant lead consulting services in the Bay Area for over 20 years. As a former contractor, performing lead-related construction work starting in 1992, he has broad knowledge of the field.

Environmental Hazards Detection LLC was founded in 2000 to provide consulting and inspection services to homeowners, contractors, realtors and HUD grantees.

Stan was also the Director of Training for the Lead Program at City College from '93 - '98, and taught lead-related construction classes for many years.
